How We Work

A Partnership, By Design.

Trinova is not a conventional company with a payroll — it is a partnership. We bring together independent professionals, each an established sole proprietor running their own practice, who choose to work together under one shared Swiss standard. The model is deliberate: it lets us pair an on‑the‑ground Swiss presence on the Riviera with dedicated near‑shore capability hubs, and it places genuine specialists — not job descriptions — at the heart of every engagement.

Each partner contributes their own expertise and remains their own principal; Trinova provides the standards, the platform and the shared brand that hold the work together. Between owners and their banks on one side, and guests, tenants and partners on the other, Trinova is the precise, accountable operator in the middle — combining Swiss precision with global reach, and increasingly powered by its own applied‑AI practice.

Réka Raffai

Founder, Partner & Global Managing Director

Connect on LinkedIn TriNova on LinkedIn

Réka Raffai is Founder, Partner and Global Managing Director of TriNova Helvetic Group. She leads the Group’s international operations across hospitality, real estate development and business services, translating strategic priorities into structured plans and coordinated execution.

With more than 14 years of experience in hospitality, restaurants and catering, Réka’s international career spans Budapest, London, Norway and Switzerland. Her background includes experience connected to Zenit Hotel, Le Méridien / Ritz‑Carlton, Michelin‑starred Restaurant Benares and Son Bryggerhus, together with a family background in the catering sector.

At TriNova, her remit encompasses operational and financial planning, hospitality and property operations, regulatory implementation, stakeholder engagement and team organisation. Combining international sector experience with hands‑on executive leadership, Réka connects strategy, people and execution across the Group’s activities.

Réka also drives TriNova’s AI enablement and digital‑first operating model — the capability that sets the Group apart. Under her leadership, TriNova has built applied‑AI capabilities spanning the design and deployment of AI agents, the design and deployment of the Group’s websites, and deep Microsoft 365 expertise creating Power Automate–based workflows that streamline operations end to end.
