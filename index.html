How We Work

A Partnership, By Design.

TriNova is not a conventional company with a payroll — it is a partnership. We bring together independent professionals, each an established sole proprietor running their own practice, who choose to work together under one shared Swiss standard. Each specialist works on their own account, chooses their own methods and equipment, is free to serve other clients, and is engaged under an independent services agreement — not a contract of employment. The model is deliberate: it lets us pair an on‑the‑ground Swiss presence on the Riviera with dedicated near‑shore capability hubs, and it places genuine specialists — not job descriptions — at the heart of every engagement.

Each partner contributes their own expertise and remains their own principal; TriNova provides the standards, the platform and the shared brand that hold the work together. Between owners and their banks on one side, and guests, tenants and partners on the other, TriNova is the precise, accountable operator in the middle — combining Swiss precision with global reach, and increasingly powered by its own applied‑AI practice.
